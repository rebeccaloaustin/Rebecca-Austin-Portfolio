About

I am an innovative, people-driven full stack software engineer with a passion for executing solutions that prioritize users. With my psychology degree, I take pride in creating applications that are intuitive, user friendly, and clean in their design. In my previous career in the hospitality industry, I refined my expertise in providing efficient and memorable customer service. I am naturally very creative, a fast learner, and I care about the quality of job that I do-whether I am building a website, debugging an app, or serving you your favorite meal, I will always strive to provide the best experience imaginable. As a mother of 2 toddlers and having grown up in a large family of 15 children, I've become a master in working with all kinds of personalities in problem solving, finding solutions, and effectively communicating those solutions.

My journey into tech has been a diverse and transformative one, shaped by my varied experiences along the way. Growing up in the Philippines, I learned the value of hard work and resilience, traits that have become foundational in my pursuit of new challenges.

After completing my degree in psychology and attending two years of nursing school, I embraced the role of stay-at-home mom to my two young children. During this time, I sought opportunities to expand my skill set and knowledge by enrolling in General Assembly's software engineering bootcamp, where I honed my technical abilities and discovered a passion for coding.

Now, armed with a unique blend of life experiences and technical knowledge, I'm poised to embark on a career in tech, driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to continuous growth and learning.
